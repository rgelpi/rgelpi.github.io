Rebekah Gelpi

Graduate Student

I am a graduate student in the Department of Psychology at the University of Toronto. I work with Dr. Daphna Buchsbaum in the Computational Cognitive Development Lab and Dr. Wil Cunningham in the Social Cognitive Science Lab. I am interested in applying computational and behavioural methods to help us understand cognitive and social development. I am particularly interested in how children and adults develop and change their beliefs, especially when the beliefs in question hold social value.
