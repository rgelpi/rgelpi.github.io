Introduction

In this experiment, you're going to see some trays with some blocks on them. The goal is to figure out which trays make activate and which trays do not activate a machine.

You will do this by seeing what happens when we put different trays on top of a machine that always activates when the right kind of tray is placed on it, and never activates when the wrong kind of tray is placed on it.

Make sure your sound is turned on.
Please do not use a mobile device for this experiment.
